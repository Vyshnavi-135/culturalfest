Rules and Regulations

  • All Engineering College Students are eligible.
  • Regitstrations are accepted online until 13 Feb 2024 | Spot Registrations are allowed until 10:30 AM, 15 Feb 2024 and events will commence from 11:00 AM.
  • Every participant must carry a valid ID card of their Institution and teams must submit an authorization letter that is duly signed by the Principal & Physical Director along with college seal during registration.
  • Winners and Runners will be awarded with cash prizes, memento/medal and appreciation certificates in the valedictory function of COLORIDO.
  • All matches are Knockout basis.
  • Judges/Referees decision will be final and violation of the rules will lead to disqualification.
